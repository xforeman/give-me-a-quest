Human Spells - Orc Spells

Human Spells
  Paladins

Holy Vision: Use this spell to see the enemy's territory. It covers a large area but only last a couple seconds or less 70

Healing: With this spell you can heal your wounded back to health 6 per 1 HP

Exorcism: Being used against the undead (Death Knights and Skeletons) the unit will either die or become wounded 4 per 1 HP

  Mages

Fireball: Like the fire from a dragon, this will wound anything in it's path 100

Flame Shield: Creates a shield of fire around a unit decreasing the health points of those around it, including buildings 80

Slow: The unit this spell is cast on will become slowed down in moving or attacking. This is the counter-spell for bloodlust 50

Invisibility: Cast this spell on a unit and they will be invisible until they either attack, harvest or cast a spell 200

Blizzard: Blizzard is very effective over a large enemy force if used correctly. This will kill land, air and sea units and can destroy buildings 25

Polymorph: The transforming of a unit into a critter is useful, though it uses a lot of mana. Use it against Dragons or Gryphons 200

Orc Spells
  Ogre Mages

Eye of Kilrogg: You can use this spell to look on enemy forces but it only last for a certain amount of time and can only see so far 70

Bloodlust: This is one of the most useful Orc spells. This will make a unit attack with more strength. It's counter-spell is slow. Be sure to use this spell if you have it!! 50

Runes: This is a blinking red trap can be laid for the enemy. It will cause a lot of damage to anyone who passes over it 200

  Death Knights

Death Coil: Drain the health of your opponent by using this spell. Taking away half of the enemy's health points will add that amount to your Death Knight's health 100

Haste: Using this spell is an advantage over your enemy. This will allow a unit to speed up in anything that unit does. (I don't know about building buildings) 50

Unholy Armor: With only taking away half of the health your unit will be invinsible to any attack for a short time. This does not work with Sappers/Demo Squads 100

Death and Decay: This is used just like Blizzard. Use it to kill a large enemy force or towers and buildings 25

Whirlwind: A whirlwind that is uncontrolable once it is cast will travel around an area wounding or destroying anything in it's path. This lasts a while 100

Raise Dead: Bring units back from the dead and have them under your control. Even your opponent's dead body can be restored under your command. They become skeletons with little health and strength and over time their health goes down and they die 50
