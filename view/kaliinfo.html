A lot of this stuff has probably changed since the last time I played on Kali (summer of '97).

Abreviations for Maps:

- FOC  Fierce Ocean Combat
- GOW  Garden of War
- HSC  High Seas Combat
- POS  Plains of Snow

Other Rules and Abbreviations:

- BRB  Be Right Back. If you don't have to say this then you won't fall behind (unless you pause)
- BTC  Back to Chat. Same as RTC (Return to Chat)
- FFA  Free For All. Everyone vs. everyone. You may have secret allies unless its only a 3 player FFA
- GL GP  Good Luck, Going Private. If you don't have an ally you would just say: GL
- LVR  Left vs. Right. The people on the left side of the map against the people on the right. You can either wait and explore to find out who is on your team, or just make a rule that everyone has to say what side they are on when they get in the game.
- Ogre Rules  This rule is kinda lame. You can't attack anyone until you have ogres (knights).
- RTC  Return to Chatroom. Game creater can RTC if someone was dropped once the game started or other in other situations.
- THF  Town Hall First. You have to build your Town Hall first before any other building. Prevents grunt rushing.
- TVB  Top vs. Bottom. Top against bottom people in the chatroom
