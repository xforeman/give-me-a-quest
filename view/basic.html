  • Cannon Towers
    Build a cannon tower in your base by your mine early in the game. Then build another cannon tower in range of the other so that when the enemy comes to attack your peons, your cannon towers can defend them. If they attack one cannon tower, the other cannon tower can defend it.
    Unless they split up their guys (some attacking one tower, and some attack the other) it should work pretty well.

  • Lumber Bug
    The lumber bug is very important when playing a low resources game. How to do it:
    Click on trees before making: Town Hall, Lumber Mill
    Then build TH or LM. You don't have to start chopping the trees for it to work, just harvest or right click on the trees.
    What it does: It gives you 100 lumber when you make either a TH or LM.

  • Repair Buildings
    This isnt repairing when your building is about to be destroyed (although, thats a good idea!). A good idea is to repair your barracks on a land level while it is being built. Buildings that need to be built up fast (like a barracks, lumber mill, shipyard, etc...) should be repaired while being built.
